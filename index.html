Chapter I:Change for the better

I.

Just because its over,
doesn't mean it didnt happen.
Just because the love is over,
doesn't mean you weren't loved.

II.

A story isn't over until,
it stops being told.

III.

Sometimes there is good days,
and sometimes there is bad days.
but most of the time,
the good days have a little bad
and the bad days have a little good.

IV.

You can't love someone into loving you.
Sometimes you just have to wait for the right person
to love you back.

V.

Sometimes it's easier to be angry
than to be sad,
and I think sometimes
we forget there is a diffrence.

VI.

I take hot showers now.
Not warm, but hot.
Not because the water feels cold,
but because I want to feel the warmth we used to have.

VII.

Confidence isn't thinking
you are better than everyone else.
It's know you are the best version of yourself.

VIII.

One person is all it took,
But one person is also all it takes.

IX.

Rain is only good when your under the awning.

X.

Even if you were not here to stay,
I'm glad your soul stopped by.

XI.

I soon realise,
the person I once loved was dead.
Not because you no longer live,
but because your soul has changed.

XII.

Your were My first,
and I don't mean first kiss
or first relationship
or first 'I love you'

You were the first person to make me feel.

XIII.

Feeling are fleeting,
just surface level,
but emotions.
Emotions are deep.
You never forget them.

XIII.

Love is a choice.
It has to be,
because if love wasn't a choice,
then you never loved me at all.

XIV.

I keep going not to prove them wrong,
not because I want to.
I keep going because,
I deserve one person
that won't give up on me.

XV.

I want people to tell me they care.
I want to know that they are proud of me.
But I can't bring myself to ask,
because if they did I shouldn't have to ask.

XVI.

I like being alone.
Thats why I knew it was you,
because in the first time in a long time
I want someones company more than my own

XVII.

Last night you asked me why I was sad.
I told you some times you just are.
I lied.
I was sad because I remebered that you didn't love me.

XVIII.

I'm not anyones first choice,
thats why I have to be my own.

XIX.

I wish people could feel how I feel,
the depth,
the vividness,
the heavy nights,
all of it.
Just so they know how much I feel.

XX.

People say our time was short.
They're not wrong,
it was.
But thats what was so amazing.
You made me feel how no one else has every made me feel,
and just in our short time.

XXI.

right now,

and I'd really love to be happy with you,

XXIX.

Brick walls are there for a reason.

At the same time,

Chapter II:Was she the one?

I.

Why do I worry about the fish,
when there are is so much of the ocean to explore.

II.

What would it be like if you didn't hold back.
What would it be like if you shared your entire self with the world.
What would it be like if you moved through the fear.

III.

All she really needed was to be free.

IV.

when we stop running to catch the train of existence,
the train of life shows up right on time.

V.

Knowing that one day we will no longer be
kids,
teens,
adults,
and eventually
no longer living
is what makes the moments and memories
so much more special than any other
indefinite illusion.
